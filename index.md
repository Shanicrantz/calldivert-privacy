# Privacy Policy - Call Divert Scheduler

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Introduction

Call Divert Scheduler ("the App") is developed and maintained by Rajdhani Telecom. This privacy policy describes how we handle your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Phone Number Data
- The App stores phone numbers you configure for call forwarding **locally on your device only**.
- Phone numbers are never transmitted to any external server.

### Schedule Data
- Your forwarding schedules (times, days, labels) are stored in a local database on your device.
- This data never leaves your device.

### Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| CALL_PHONE | Required to execute USSD codes (*21*number#) for activating/deactivating call forwarding |
| READ_PHONE_STATE | Required to check current call forwarding status |
| RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED | Required to reschedule alarms after device restart |
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS | Required to show notifications when forwarding is active |
| SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM | Required for precise scheduling of forwarding start/stop times |

## Data Storage
- All data is stored **locally on your device** using an encrypted Room database.
- No data is transmitted to external servers.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## Data Deletion
- You can delete any schedule within the app.
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date.
